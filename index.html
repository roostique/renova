1. Introduction

Renovation Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal productivity app that helps you plan, track, and export renovation projects. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how it is stored, and your rights as a user.

Short version: Renovation Calculator does not collect or transmit any personal data to external servers. Everything you enter stays on your device.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All data created inside the app is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and the device file system. This includes:

  • Projects & items ‚Äî names, descriptions, costs, categories, completion status, and dates you enter.
  • Project photos ‚Äî images you optionally attach to projects, stored as compressed JPEG data on-device.
  • Custom categories ‚Äî any category names and emoji you create.
  • App preferences ‚Äî chosen language (English / Russian / Kazakh), currency, color theme, and export count for the free tier.
  • Review tracking counters ‚Äî a local count of app launches and items added, used only to decide when to prompt the native iOS "Rate the App" dialog. This data never leaves your device.

None of this data is synced to iCloud, transmitted to our servers, or shared with any third party.

3. In-App Purchases (PRO Subscription)

Renovation Calculator offers an optional PRO subscription that unlocks premium themes, unlimited exports, and full category management. Purchases are processed entirely through Apple StoreKit. We never see, store, or handle your payment card details.

Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by the Apple Privacy Policy. The only information we receive from StoreKit is a verified transaction record confirming whether a valid subscription is active ‚Äî no payment details, no Apple ID, no personal identifiers.

4. Feedback

The in-app feedback form opens your device's native Mail app via a mailto: link pre-filled with your message. We do not intercept, store, or process the message before it reaches your Mail app.

When you choose to send an email, the content you type and your email address (as configured in your Mail app) will be sent to rustik7252@gmail.com. This information is used only to respond to your feedback and is not shared with anyone.

5. Exported Files (PDF, CSV, JSON)

The export feature generates files from your local project data and saves them to a temporary directory on your device. Files are shared only when you explicitly use the iOS share sheet ‚Äî for example, to save to Files, send via AirDrop, or email to yourself. We have no access to files you export or where you send them.

6. No Analytics or Tracking

Renovation Calculator contains no third-party analytics SDKs (such as Firebase, Mixpanel, or Amplitude), no advertising frameworks, and no crash-reporting services. We do not track your behaviour in the app, build user profiles, or monetise your data in any way.
